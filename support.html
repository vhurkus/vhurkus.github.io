Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a product?

Tap the "+" button on the Vault tab. You can add product details manually or scan a receipt using your camera.

How do warranty reminders work?

When you add a product with a warranty end date, the app automatically schedules reminders at 30 days, 7 days, and 1 day before expiration. Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings and tap "Export Data". You can share the exported file via email, AirDrop, or save it to Files.

How do I import data from a backup?

Go to Settings and tap "Import Data". Select your backup file and the app will restore your saved products.

Is my data stored in the cloud?

No. All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit any personal information.

How do I change the app language?

Go to Settings and tap the language option at the top. MyWarrantyVault supports English, Turkish, German, French, Spanish, Japanese, Korean, and Simplified Chinese.
