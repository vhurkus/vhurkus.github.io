Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Huseyin Yucel ("we", "us", or "our") provides Gravity Grid Puzzle as a game for iOS. This policy explains how the app handles information.

Information Collection and Use

Gravity Grid Puzzle does not require an account and we do not directly collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other contact information through the app.

Local Game Progress

The app may save game progress, level completion, settings, and best star results locally on your device. This information stays on your device and is not sent to us.

Advertising and Rewards

The app uses Google AdMob to provide optional rewarded ads for assists such as hints, undo, and extra moves. Rewarded ads are requested only after you choose to use an ad-supported assist. You can continue playing without watching rewarded ads.

Third-Party Data Processing

Google AdMob and related Google SDKs may process information such as device identifiers, advertising data, coarse location, product interaction data, crash data, performance data, and other diagnostic data to provide ads, measure ad performance, prevent abuse, personalize ads where permitted, and maintain app functionality.

Depending on your region and device settings, the app may ask for advertising consent and Apple's App Tracking Transparency permission before requesting personalized ads. You can change tracking permission later in iOS Settings.

Google's use of data is described in Google's policies at https://policies.google.com/privacy and https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Accounts and Sign-In

The app does not require an account, sign-in, email address, phone number, or other personal identifier to play.

Game Center

If you choose to use Apple Game Center, Apple may process Game Center information under Apple's own privacy policy. Game Center is optional and is not required to play the game.

Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
