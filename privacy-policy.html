Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Huseyin Yucel ("we", "us", or "our") built the MyWarrantyVault app (the "App") as a free application. This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information if anyone decides to use our App.

Information Collection and Use

MyWarrantyVault does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data you enter into the App, including product information, warranty details, and receipt images, is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to any of your data.

Data Storage

All warranty data, product information, and images are stored exclusively on your device using local storage. No data is sent to any server or cloud service. Your data remains entirely under your control.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services that collect information used to identify you. We do not integrate any analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks.

Camera and Photo Access

The App may request access to your device's camera and photo library solely for the purpose of scanning receipts and adding product images. These images are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared with any third party.

Notifications

The App may send local notifications to remind you about upcoming warranty expirations. These notifications are generated locally on your device and do not involve any external server communication.

Data Export

The App provides the ability to export warranty data as PDF files and to backup/restore your data. These operations are performed locally, and the resulting files remain on your device unless you choose to share them.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
