Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Tap tiles to change the board state and complete each field before your moves run out.

Does the game require an account?

No. Gravity Grid Puzzle does not require sign-in or account creation.

Can I play offline?

Yes. The game works offline after installation.

How does star scoring work?

Stars are awarded based on how efficiently you complete a level. Your best result is kept.

System Requirements

Gravity Grid Puzzle requires iOS 17.0 or later.
